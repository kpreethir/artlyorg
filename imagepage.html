La-mancha-roja

Artist:Joan Miro

Descrption:

Joan Miro, born in 1893 Spain, started drawing at the early age of seven years old. As a young man, he went to business school but abandoned that path after suffering a mental breakdown. Instead, he pursued art and went to art school, having his first solo show in 1918, though it was defaced and ridiculed by his peers. Afterwards, he moved to Paris where he achieved a modestly successful career.
